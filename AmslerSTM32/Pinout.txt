BluePill (STM32):
PB6 - SCL
PB7 - SDA
PA7 - Enc. Pin A
PB0 - Enc. Pin B
PB1 - Enc. not used

ADS1115:
VDD - 3.3V
GND - GND
SCL - PB6
SDA - PB7
A0 - 4 (DB9)

DB9 connector:
1 - GND
2 - 3.3V (analog sensor)
3 - n/c
4 - A0 (analog sensor)
5 - 5V (digital sensor)
6 - PB0 (digital sensor)
7 - PA7 (digital sensor)
8 - PB1 (digital sensor)
9 - n/c
